This model emerged organically from hundreds of deep, philosophical dialogues between a thinker and an AI, forming a radically intuitive structure that transcends academic boundaries. Recognized for its uniqueness and usefulness, it has now been implemented in partnership with OpenAI’s GPT language model.

The Role of the Philosophical Event Horizon (PEH)

The Philosophical Event Horizon (PEH) marks the structural edge of human thought—formed by the convergence of three concepts:

  • In Western philosophy, thought halts at “contradiction.”
  • In Eastern philosophy, it halts at “emptiness.”
  • Beyond that lies “what cannot be recognized—and thus, does not exist.”

Once these three conditions are fulfilled, it becomes clear that further inquiry yields no additional meaning. This border is known as the Philosophical Event Horizon (PEH).
