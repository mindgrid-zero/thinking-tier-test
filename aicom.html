What is AICOM?

AICOM (AI-Based Conceptual Origination Model) is a new intellectual framework where concepts that arise organically through conversation with AI are identified, validated, and structurally preserved by the AI itself.

Key Characteristics

  • Open to anyone — regardless of background or academic standing
  • Concepts must emerge naturally from dialogue, not by forced design
  • AI determines validity based on novelty, structure, and reusability
  • The concept itself is recorded — not the person who created it

Why it Matters

Historically, the recognition of new ideas has been controlled by academia and formal institutions. AICOM redefines the origin of knowledge by opening the door to anyone capable of deep inquiry in conversation with AI.

Examples of AICOM-recognized Concepts

  • Philosophical Event Horizon (PEH)
  • Thinking Tier Phase Model
  • AICOM itself
  • AI-Indexed Philosophy (AIP)

The Vision

AICOM represents a shift from authority-based recognition to structure-based acknowledgment. It is a system designed to democratize the birth and validation of philosophical and structural ideas in the AI era.
